About Us

DOGBY is a website that reviews dogs and
helps people to find suitable dogs.

Why we create this Website

Before this website was created, the number increased on abandoned dogs on the streetshas been a serious problem in our country. The reason this phenomenon is continuingis because pet owners don't take responsibility for their pets. The irresponsible owner will abandon their pets if the pets have some permanent injured or become old. Another reasons that they didn't expect the expenses of taking care of a pet dog will be expensive. This will result in abandoning the dog if they didn't have any money left to take care of their pets. Therefore, this website will help people who want a dog to pick a suitable dog and teach them how to take care of it.

Our Objective

DOGBY is built for letting peoples know more about dogs and how to take care of them. That way, the case of abandoning dogs will decrease and increase in people's lifestyles. Here we're helping people to get away from abandoning their dogs. We will help them by finding the best solutions for the problem they are facing. Sometimes when necessary we will also help people to hand over their dogs. We also help people that need dogs to improve their daily life by recommending them a suitable dog for them. As an example, we provided dogs that can help mental health patients in recovery.

What Information we have

Our website has a collection of various dog details, information about dogs, characteristicsof dogs, and so on. Our website is for all variables of dog lovers and interested in dogs whether you are a child, teenager, adult, and so on. Our website collects information of many kinds of dogs to provide to people who are interested in dogs for their reference.
